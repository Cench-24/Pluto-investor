SYSTEM ADMINISTRATORS

Who are the System admins? The System Administrators also known as sysadmins, play a crucial role in maintaining and operating computer systems or networks for organizations. Their responsibilities are wide-ranging and can vary from one organization to another. Here are some key aspects of what system administrators do:

1. Upkeep and configuration : Sysadmins are responsible for ensuring the reliable operation of computer systems, especially multi-user computers like servers. They set up, configure, and maintain these systems.

2. Installation and Upgrade : Acquiring, installing, and upgrading computer components and software.

3. Security Policy : Maintaining security policies, Identifying and resolving issues.
