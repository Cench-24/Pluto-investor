HELPDESK

I understand HelpDesk to be a first level support team of the IT department that provides technical support to the end users. Issues Such as hardware and software related problems are resolved by the HelpDesk team.

I understand that the HelpDesk team is responsible for the resolution of all issues that are reported by the end users to the IT department.

These issues can be reported using the Sysaid, email, calls or face to face.
