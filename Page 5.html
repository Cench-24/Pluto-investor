IT SECURITY

IT Security encompasses a range of practical and technologies designed to protect digital information and data from unauthorized access, disclosure, modification, destruction, and loss.They also included measures to protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of data.

IT Security include:
Access Control: Limiting access to information and resources to authorized user having Access to sensitive data.

Compliance:
Compliance is the process of ensuring that an organization meets its legal obligations. Reducing potential legal and financial consequence.

Data Security:
Data security is the protection of data from unauthorized access, disclosure, modification, destruction, and loss.
