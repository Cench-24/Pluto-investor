BUSINESS INFORMATION COORDINATORS

I understand Business Information Coordinators to be a second level support team of the IT department.

The Business Information Coordinators are responsible for managing the business information of the organization.

Here are some key aspects of what Business Information Coordinators do:

1. Business Information Management :

The Business Information Coordinators are responsible for managing the business information of the organization.

2. Business Application Development :

The Business Information Coordinators are responsible for development and strategic build of app giving using streamline or flow chats for the apps team to work on the business information of the organization.
